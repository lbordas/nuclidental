What are dental implants?

Dental implants are artificial roots that are surgically placed in the jaw to replace missing teeth. They are designed to fuse with the bone, creating a stable and durable base to support the new teeth.

Dental implants look and feel like natural teeth, allowing you to eat, speak and smile with confidence.

What to expect during the dental implants treatment?

The dental implants treatment is a simple and safe procedure that normally includes the following steps:

Free consultation and evaluation

Our implantologist will examine your teeth, gums and jawbone and discuss your oral health history and goals. We will also perform scans of your mouth to create a personalized treatment plan. This first consultation/assessment is free.

Dental Implant Placement

The implantologist will surgically place the dental implants into the jawbone under local anesthesia or sedation. You may experience some swelling or discomfort for a few days after the procedure, but it can be controlled with pain medication and ice packs.

Healing and bone integration

Dental implants take several months to fuse with the bone and create a stable base. During this time, you will wear a temporary crown or bridge and follow our post-operative oral hygiene instructions.

Restoration

Once the dental implants have fully integrated with the bone, our dentist will place a custom crown or bridge on the implants, restoring your smile and function.
